Helwan University Thesis

Helwan University is one of the Egyptian public universities working on:

  • Providing high-quality academic programs, governed by local and international standards, to prepare a distinguished graduate capable of competition and creativity, meeting the needs of society, and contributing to its development and well-being.
  • Providing programs to raise the efficiency of faculty members, and develop their educational and research capabilities, so that this reflects positively on both the student and the local and national environment.
